# LowQ Commercial License — Draft Terms Sheet

Commercial use of LowQ requires a written commercial license.

## Commercial license may include

- enterprise deployment rights,
- hosted service rights,
- marketplace deployment rights,
- internal AI infrastructure optimization rights,
- pilot support,
- telemetry bridge support,
- certificate/reporting integration,
- warranty/support terms if negotiated.

## Prohibited without commercial license

- using LowQ to optimize paid AI services,
- embedding LowQ in a commercial product,
- reselling LowQ reports or certificates,
- offering LowQ as managed software,
- using LowQ in production data-center optimization,
- presenting LowQ results as an enterprise service deliverable.

## Suggested commercial tiers

1. **Pilot License** — 14 to 30 day evaluation, limited environment, limited reports.
2. **Internal Deployment License** — use within one organization.
3. **Enterprise License** — multi-site, support, telemetry integration, custom reporting.
4. **OEM / Marketplace License** — embedded or resale use.
