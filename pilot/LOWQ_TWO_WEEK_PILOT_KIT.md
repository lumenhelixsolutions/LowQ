# LowQ Two-Week Waste-Heat Assessment

## Goal
Determine whether LowQ can reduce measured or estimated compute waste, power burden, thermal shadow, or cost for a defined AI workload.

## Week 1

Day 1: intake and workload selection.  
Day 2: baseline proof run.  
Day 3: telemetry import or sensor setup.  
Day 4: LowQ optimized proof run.  
Day 5: initial certificate and ROI report.

## Week 2

Day 6–7: repeat testing.  
Day 8: variance and quality review.  
Day 9: executive ROI report.  
Day 10: deployment/no-deployment recommendation.

## Required inputs

- workload sample,
- model/runtime,
- hardware profile,
- telemetry source if available,
- success criteria,
- operational constraints.

## Deliverables

- LowQ Certificate,
- ROI report,
- telemetry quality report,
- benchmark summary,
- pilot recommendation.

## Success criteria

Proceed if evidence shows credible reduction in workload, runtime, energy, or thermal burden with quality preserved.
