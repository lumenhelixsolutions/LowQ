# LowQ Claim Guardrails

## Approved language

- LowQ reduces computational waste before it becomes heat.
- LowQ estimates possible energy/thermal savings using workload and telemetry data.
- LowQ exports proof certificates that distinguish estimates, simulations, telemetry, and formal measurement.
- In this run, LowQ reduced tokens by X% and estimated joules by Y% under S0 assumptions.

## Avoid unless proven

- LowQ reduces data-center heat by X%.
- LowQ guarantees energy savings.
- LowQ replaces cooling systems.
- LowQ directly lowers GPU temperature without telemetry.
- LowQ is validated for all AI workloads.

## Mandatory caveat for S0/S1

This is a simulation-backed workload proof, not direct hardware heat measurement.

## Mandatory caveat for telemetry claims

Results apply to this hardware, workload, model, configuration, and measurement method.
