# LowQ — One-Page Product Brief

**LowQ is an AI infrastructure optimization layer that reduces wasteful computation, memory movement, redundant context processing, and inefficient workload routing to lower the heat and energy burden of AI systems at the source.**

## Why now
AI infrastructure is becoming power- and heat-constrained. Most solutions focus on cooling after heat exists. LowQ targets waste before it becomes heat.

## What LowQ does

- reduces avoidable workload size,
- detects redundant context and repeated work,
- supports simulation and telemetry-backed proof,
- exports LowQ Certificates,
- translates results into ROI reports,
- supports pilot validation.

## Product modules

- LowQ Lab,
- LowQ ROI Calculator,
- LowQ Certificate,
- LowQ Engine,
- LowQ Bridge,
- LowQ SimTwin,
- LowQ Pilot.
